Information Systems

Information Systems is a blend of business and technology. Learn to create, manage, and analyze data systems used for business intelligence, security, and business operations. A compliment of Information Technology that focuses more on the application of information stored in databases.

Historically, one hundred percent of information systems students seeking jobs have accepted offers within three months of graduation. Information systems graduates are employed all over the country, working for both multinational firms and startups. Take a look at the career resources page to learn more about careers in Information Systems. Also, take a look at the career explorer to see where graduates of this program have begun their careers.

The Masters in Information Systems Managment offers an integrated Bachelors & Masters program which takes about 5 years to complete.
